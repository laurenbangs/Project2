As a student here going to the dining halls and making your own food gets pretty old fast. Thankfully Athens has many local places to eat. Top Five Places to Eat in Athens, Ohio

After spending four years in Athens I have finally found my favorite places to eat.

#5 Jackie O’s Brewery and restaurant

Saul Goodman

Ceo & Food Critic

I really like Pigskin. Very good fries and chips. On Tuesday they have 1/2 off burgers which are very good. On Wednesdays, they have a wing deal. There wings are pretty good. I love their barbecue sauce and thought it was good on the wings but also liked dipping my chips and fries into it.

Sara Wilsson

Passing Through Athens

You know this place is good stuff when you have to wait in a line that formed before they open. With a little customizing, this place has many vegan options to offer. One bagel is filling, but I got two just because the prices were so nice! I recommend the Caribbean Queen and Kimmie Gibbler. My husband also enjoyed his Chris Farley and Kung Fu. - Bagel Street Deli

Jena Karlis

Student

Overall, great spot to grab some food and drinks. I was in the mood for some white sauce pizza, so I ordered the oestrike and it did the trick. Simple but good pizza. Jackie O's is a staple of Athens, so you can't go wrong coming here!- Jackie O's

Matt Brandon

Student

This is the place where I feel like HOME, the BEST Chinese food period. In the middle of Ohio this restaurant really is the best food for anyone that love and appreciate Chinese Food and Sushi, forgot about Chipotle, THIS IS the place you NEED to go!!!- Ginger

John Larson

Professor

Awesome local small business! They've got a TON of options to choose from. Located in uptown Athens on Court Street just steps away from college green. Brenen's is the perfect place to grab a coffee and sandwich for lunch. Items on their menu are totally customizable and delicious!- Brennans Coffee
